Our Vision

CCS ProjectHub aims to transform how programming students share and learn from each other's work. Unlike traditional learning management systems that focus solely on submissions and grades, ProjectHub creates a collaborative environment where creativity is celebrated and innovation is encouraged.

We believe that seeing how others solve problems and structure their code is one of the most powerful ways to learn programming. ProjectHub makes this possible by turning every submission into a learning opportunity for the entire CCS community.
