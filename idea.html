CCS ProjectHub: An Online Platform for Showcasing and Submitting Programming Outputs

The Problem

Students currently submit project outputs individually through email or MOLE, and their work remains unseen by others. This creates several challenges:

The Solution

The CCS ProjectHub is a web-based platform designed for students of the College of Computer Studies at MSU-IIT to submit, showcase, and explore programming projects across major subjects. Unlike MOLE, which mainly focuses on assignment submissions and grading, ProjectHub serves as both a learning hub and a digital portfolio space where students can publicly display their works and learn from their peers.

Teachers can view, evaluate, and highlight outstanding projects, while students can explore their peers' work for inspiration and learning. This transforms programming submissions into a shared learning environment where creativity and innovation are recognized.

Key Features

  • Student Dashboard: Upload and manage submissions for each subject or lab activity with an intuitive interface that organizes projects by course and semester.
  • Project Showcase: Publicly display approved outputs for others to view or learn from, creating a gallery of student work that inspires creativity.
  • Instructor Dashboard: Create lab activities, view submissions, and record grades with streamlined tools designed for efficient evaluation.
  • Project Cards: Each project has its own card displaying the student’s name, project title, description, and a link to the deployed site or repository.
  • Search and Filter: Easily browse projects by subject, programming language, semester, or technology stack to find relevant examples.
  • Responsive Design: Access the platform seamlessly from desktop, tablet, or mobile devices for maximum flexibility.
  • Public Showcase Feature: Turns submissions into learning resources for future students.
  • Optional Peer Feedback: Students can comment or react to classmates’ projects to foster collaboration and constructive discussion.

What Makes It Different from MOLE and Google Classroom?

While MOLE focuses mainly on assignment submission and grading, ProjectHub emphasizes visibility, collaboration, and learning from peers.
Key differentiators include:

  • Public showcase feature that turns submissions into learning resources.
  • Encourages peer feedback and interaction among students.
  • Displays project details such as repository links and descriptions.
  • Creates a searchable gallery of student work by subject or semester.
