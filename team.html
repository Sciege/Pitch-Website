About our Team

CCS ProjectHub is being developed by a dedicated team of Computer Studies students at MSU-IIT who recognized the need for a better way to share and showcase programming projects. Our team combines technical expertise with a deep understanding of the challenges students face in collaborative learning environments.

We are committed to creating a platform that not only meets academic requirements but also provides real value to the CCS community by fostering peer learning and celebrating student creativity.
